About

Quality education rooted in care, discipline, and growth.

ASISH PUBLIC SCHOOL, G. Udayagiri, Kandhamal, Odisha, is an ICSE-curriculum school established in 1993. The school is committed to building a strong academic foundation while encouraging curiosity, confidence, values, and personal responsibility.

Our learning environment is designed to be safe, supportive, and inspiring. Students are guided to think clearly, communicate well, respect others, and develop the habits needed for lifelong learning.

With a focus on meaningful classroom learning and overall development, ASISH PUBLIC SCHOOL works with families to help every child grow with discipline, kindness, and purpose.
